About Mauli Decoration

At Mauli Decoration, we don’t just decorate spaces—we tell stories. We specialize in the dual art of Event Management and Luxury Interior Design, blending the thrill of a perfectly executed celebration with the lasting beauty of 3D interior wall art.

From the breathtaking floral arrangements of a wedding to the cutting-edge textures of modern 3D wallpaper, our mission is to turn the ordinary into the extraordinary.

We believe that every environment, whether a temporary stage or a permanent home, should be a reflection of style, emotion, and innovation.

Our Services

Birthday Party Setup

We create vibrant and memorable birthday party setups tailored to your theme and preferences. From colorful balloon decorations to beautifully designed backdrops, we ensure every detail is perfect. Our team handles everything from cake table styling to lighting and props. Whether it’s a kids’ party or an elegant celebration, we bring creativity and joy to every event. Let us turn your special day into an unforgettable experience filled with happiness and style.

Engagement & Reception Events

We specialize in creating elegant and memorable engagement and reception events with beautifully designed setups and premium decorations. Our team focuses on delivering a perfect blend of modern style and traditional charm to match your special occasion. From stage design and floral arrangements to lighting and seating, every detail is carefully planned. We ensure a smooth and stress-free experience so you can enjoy every moment of your celebration. Our goal is to turn your event into a stunning and unforgettable experience for you and your guests.

3D Wallpaper Interior

3D wallpaper interior design adds a modern and stylish touch to any space by creating depth and a realistic visual effect on walls. It transforms plain walls into eye-catching features using textures, patterns, and creative designs. These wallpapers are available in various styles like geometric, nature, abstract, and luxury themes to match different interiors. They are easy to install, durable, and enhance the overall ambiance of living rooms, bedrooms, offices, and commercial spaces. With 3D wallpapers, you can give your space a premium and elegant look without major renovation..

Corporate Event Management

We provide professional corporate event management services tailored to meet your business needs. From conferences and seminars to product launches and corporate parties, we handle every detail with precision. Our team ensures seamless planning, elegant setups, and high-quality execution. We focus on creating a strong brand impression through well-organized and visually appealing events. With our expertise, your corporate events become impactful, engaging, and memorable.

Festival & Cultural Event Decoration

We specialize in creating vibrant and memorable festival and cultural event decorations that reflect tradition, creativity, and elegance. Our designs include colorful themes, floral arrangements, lighting setups, and customized stage decor to match every celebration. From traditional festivals like Diwali and Ganpati to cultural programs and community events, we bring authenticity and festive spirit to every setup. We focus on high-quality materials, unique concepts, and detailed execution to enhance the overall experience. Our goal is to transform your event into a visually stunning and joyful celebration that leaves a lasting impression.

Stage Design

Stage design plays a crucial role in creating a visually appealing and memorable event experience. It combines elements like lighting, backdrop, colors, and props to match the theme of the occasion. A well-designed stage enhances the overall ambiance and becomes the focal point for guests and photography. Creative use of balloons, flowers, and LED lighting adds elegance and modern appeal to the setup. Professional stage design ensures your event looks organized, attractive, and truly unforgettable.
